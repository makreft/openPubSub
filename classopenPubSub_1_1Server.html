The PublishedDataSet (PDS) and PubSubConnection are the toplevel entities and can exist alone. The PDS contains the collection of the published fields. All other PubSub elements are directly or indirectly linked with the PDS or connection. Returns the publishedDataSetId, that is needed for adding DS fields to the PublishedDataSet.
